About Me

Hello, I’m Rihab — or simply Ri.

I have four years of experience in freelance design, and today I’ve chosen to focus on digital invitations because they are a beautiful way to express yourself and share love in a simple, elegant form.

An invitation is more than a message — it reflects your inner world and how you see the outside world through design. It’s also a heartfelt way to show someone you care about them and want them to be part of your most special moments.

Wishing you all joyful and memorable celebrations.
